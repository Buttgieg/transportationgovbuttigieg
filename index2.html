About Me

Hi, I’m Pete Buttigieg, the United States Secretary of Transportation. Since February 3, 2021, I’ve had the honor of leading the Department of Transportation, where I work every day to make transportation safer, more efficient, and accessible for everyone in our country. From modernizing our infrastructure to tackling climate change through innovative, sustainable solutions, my mission is to help shape a transportation system that meets the demands of the 21st century. I’m passionate about leveraging the transformative power of transportation to connect communities, create jobs, and build a better future for all. Before stepping into this role, I served as the Mayor of South Bend, Indiana, where I focused on revitalizing the city’s economy and updating critical infrastructure. These experiences taught me the value of resilience, innovation, and putting people first. Fun fact: I’m also a Navy veteran, a Harvard graduate, a Rhodes Scholar, and an unapologetic transportation enthusiast.
